# Tech Audits Downtown - Episode Ideas

## EP 1: The Lasting Room (Bar & Grill - Haverhill)
**Status:** Site already built, ready to film

**Shot list:**
- Walk down Washington St, approach The Lasting Room
- Quick clip inside the bar (the marble top, TVs, vibe)
- Pull out phone, show their current site - black background, red text, pizza photo
- Quick reactions / commentary on what's wrong
- Screen record montage: building the new site with AI (fast cuts, code flying)
- Side-by-side reveal: old vs new
- Walk back in and show the owner on a laptop/phone

**Key talking points:**
- This place looks amazing in person but their website makes it look like a pizza shop from 2005
- First impression for anyone googling "bars in Haverhill" is this site
- Built a full modern site in under 2 hours with AI
- Mobile-first because that's how everyone finds a bar

**Thumbnail idea:** Split screen - their old ugly site on left, new site on right, you in the middle looking shocked
